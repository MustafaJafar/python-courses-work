scrapping steps : 
    1.get web page (following site structre)
    2.parsing its text , tags
    3.get data 

difference between scrapping and crawling : 

Scrapping :  I know what I came for , and I alread know a pattern and am following it
    1.already know website pattern  , or site map
    2.know the desired data 
    3.get that data and ignore the rest

Crawling : I don't know what I came for , and I am not sure if this data is valuable or not ! 
    1.get website pattern , or recognizing site map  
    2.scrapping based on that pattern
    3.saving and manging data
